The heightmap can now be used to compute parameters that can be used for the procedural shading. The parameters that I compute is the slope of the surface point and the wind exposure of the surface point. If the slope of the surface area is high enough the snow should transition over to rock. If the normal is aligned a lot to the wind direction the snow should also transition over to rock.
